Our Mission

Join us for a unique and meaningful event that bridges psychology, sociology, cognitive science, and the real-world challenge of campus bullying. Together, we'll dive into the hidden roots of why bullying happens‚Äîhow our minds perceive others, how empathy forms, and what drives misunderstanding or harm.

This isn't just a talk‚Äîit's an interactive experience. We'll provide art materials like watercolors, brushes, and paper so you can express your thoughts and emotions creatively. Through collective reflection and artistic expression, we'll explore practical ways to prevent bullying in elementary, middle, and high schools.

Come add your voice, your story, and your ideas to a movement that goes beyond awareness‚Äîtoward real change. Let's make empathy visible, one brushstroke at a time.
